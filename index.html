What is European PLACE Conference?

The European PLACE Conference is the only biennial event in Europe focused on the extrusion coating and laminating industry featuring the latest technology and developments in raw materials, processing, inspection and applications.

The 15th TAPPI European PLACE Conference will focus on the latest trends, technologies and applications affecting the coating and lamination, film extrusion and end use.

Applications

  • Sustainability and Social Aspects of Packaging
  • Packaging and Society
  • Material Solutions; Converting Process Solutions; and Hardware Solutions
  • Process Monitoring Tools (in Production and Lab) Newest Trends and Innovations
